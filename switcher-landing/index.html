How Insurance Brokerage Works

An Insurance Transaction, Not a Medical Service

As a licensed insurance brokerage, easyKind Health LLC acts as a financial intermediary in insurance transactions. When a policyholder wants to compare Medicare Supplement insurance rates, they provide basic demographic and policy information — the same data any licensed insurance agent collects to generate a quote.

We submit that information to licensed insurance carriers, retrieve rate quotes, and present the comparison. If the buyer elects to purchase a policy, the transaction is completed as an insurance enrollment under state insurance law — a financial services transaction, not a medical service.

Medicare Supplement insurance is a private insurance product. It is not part of the Medicare program itself. It is a financial product sold by licensed insurance companies and distributed through licensed brokers and agents operating under NAIC Industry Code 524210.
